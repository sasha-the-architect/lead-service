What makes "Verified" leads different from "Basic"?
Verified leads have been personally contacted to confirm they're actively looking to hire, have a budget approved, and a timeline for their project. Basic leads are database matches that may be outdated.
How quickly will I receive leads?
Leads are delivered to your inbox every morning by 8 AM. You'll also receive SMS notifications for verified leads so you can follow up immediately.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, absolutely. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your monthly subscription at any time with no penalties or hidden fees.
What if the leads don't convert?
We stand behind our data. If a significant portion of your leads are invalid or unresponsive, we'll credit your account or provide replacement leads.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the lead quality, let us know within 30 days and we'll give you a full refund.
